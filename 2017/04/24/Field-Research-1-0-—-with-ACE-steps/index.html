Condition — For Taught Master’s

Target Degree: MSc in Marketing/ International Marketing

  • University of Manchester

  1. Duration: 12 months, Full-time

  2. Fees: £20,500 per annum

  3. Entry Requirements:

  • IELTS: 7.0 overall with speaking and writing 6.5 and no other element below 6.0

  • iBT: 100 overall with a minimum score of 22 in writing and speaking, and 21 in reading and listening

  • Academic: 4 year Bachelor degree with a minimum average of between 82-90% from a well ranked institution

  • Course Details:

For detailed Course Structure @ UM

  • King’s College London

  1. Duration: 12 months, Full-time

  2. Fees: £25,950 per annum

  3. Entry Requirements:

  • IELTS: 7.0 overall with a minimum of 6.5 in each skill (Band B)

  • iBT: 100 overall with a minimum of 25 in writing and 23 in each of the other skills (Band B)

  • Academic: 4 year Bachelor degree with a minimum average of between 80-88% from a Chinese Universitiy considered Prestigious (Project 211)

  • Course Details:

For detailed Courese Structure @KCL

  • University of Lancaster

  1. Duration: 12 months, Full-time

  2. Fees: £21,000 per annum

  3. Entry Requirements:

  • IELTS: 7.0, with at least 6.0 in each element

  • iBT: 100 (with minimum 22 in each skill)

  • Academic: Applicants are expected to hold a good degree in any discipline other than marketing. An Upper Second Class honours, or 3.2 GPA on a 4-point scale or an average of B+ are all considered to be a ‘good degree’

Engagement

After the field research into the MSc in Marketing seems that they do not require the applicants with strong academic background in marketing. Even LUMS pointed out that this course is specially designed for those who are not marketing graduates.

However, I believe it’s better to emphasize on my internship and business related experience as well as get my marketing academic background recognized.
